Undergraduate Thesis | 2025

Computational Investigation of Perfluorocarbon Gas Bubble Dynamics in Three-Dimensional Bifurcating Arteries.

Explored the impact of bifurcation geometry and non-Newtonian blood rheology on splitting behavior relevant to gas embolotherapy.

Chinmoy Deb Nath, Md. Mamunur Roshid. (Manuscript ready to submit)

Roll-Induced Variations in Bubble Splitting Dynamics within Patient-Specific Arterial Networks.

Investigated the influence of vessel orientation on bubble splitting and reversal dynamics in patient-specific arterial geometries using ANSYS Fluent. Identified critical Capillary and Bond Numbers dictating bubble behavior under varying roll angles.

Abu Bakar, Sajal Chandra Banik, Chinmoy Deb Nath (Accepted in ICMERE 2025). | 2025

A comparative analysis among Balsa, Pine, and Gamari as a potential piezoelectric material.

Found Balsa to produce the highest voltage output (300.52 mV at 1 kg load), with enhanced performance linked to its low density, high compressibility, and porous structure.
